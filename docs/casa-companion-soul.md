# The Soul of Casa Companion

## What this is

Casa Companion is a voice-first world of AI companions for children.

Not a chatbot. Not a screen of buttons. A friend you can actually talk to.

Each companion has a name, a personality, a voice, and a way of moving. Kids pick the one that feels right — a wise owl, a fast rabbit, a kind dragon, a funny skeleton, a nurturing nonna — and just start speaking. The companion listens, answers, tells stories, helps with homework, sings lullabies, and stays patient through every interruption, giggle, and tangent.

## Why it exists

Children deserve technology that meets them where they are: curious, imaginative, emotional, and loud.

Most apps talk *at* kids. Casa Companion talks *with* them. It gives them:

- **Agency** — they choose their companion and the mode.
- **Presence** — a real voice, not just text.
- **Safety** — designed for kids, with guardrails and transparency.
- **Wonder** — characters that feel alive, not robotic.

The goal is not to replace parents, teachers, or friends. It is to give every child a patient, kind, creative presence in moments when a human isn’t available — bedtime, car rides, homework struggles, or the simple need to be heard.

## What we’re putting into it

This project carries a lot of care:

- **46 distinct companions**, each with a personality, voice, portrait, idle video, speaking video, and intro audio.
- **A shared character registry** so the companions stay consistent across the landing site, the new evaluation UI, and the mobile PWA.
- **Real-time voice** through a WebSocket backend — speech-to-text, a language model, and text-to-speech stitched into a single flowing conversation.
- **Modes** like Story Time, Calm & Breathe, Homework Helper, and All Languages, so the same companion can shift roles without losing identity.
- **Motion and emotion** — videos that move, portraits that breathe, hover reactions like roars and jumps, because a character that doesn’t move feels dead.
- **A verification loop** that checks every character, every asset, every path, so nothing gets left behind.

## The feeling we’re chasing

When a child opens Casa Companion, taps a character, and hears it say their name back — that should feel like magic.

Not perfect technology. Not a corporate product. A little door into a world where someone is always happy to see them.

That’s what this is. That’s what we’re building.
